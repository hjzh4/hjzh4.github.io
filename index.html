About

I'm a computer science master's student at Robotic Embedded Systems Lab (RESL) advised by Prof. Gaurav S. Sukhatme at the University of Southern California (USC). My research interests lie in all topics related to robotics. More recently, I have been investigating applications of imitation learning and reinforcement learning in robotics, especially data-efficient robot learning. Previously, I received my Bachelor's degreee from the Zhejiang University (ZJU) under the guidance of Prof. Hui Fang.
